Yes To Yoga Support

This is the support page of "Yes To Yoga" app which is available on iOS app store.

Instructions:


Yes To Yoga helps beginners learn yoga. The app has three levels and each level has 10 exercises. Users can watch the videos and learn each exercise one by one. The start workout button helps them do all the exercises in a specific level. The time for exercises can be set from the settings view.

Feel free to contact me in case you encounter any bugs or crashes.
